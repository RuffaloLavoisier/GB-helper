## Message: Pebble: add toggle for PebbleKit support

The device setting was conflated with "third_party_apps_set_settings" in c3d76477ec9b3a10bbe0627fdc759a73322dd710, but after reviewing PebbleKitSupport class, the allowed PebbleKit actions are not capable of changing settings on the android device as all of them are just piping data to and from the Pebble device.
PebbleKit messages can change settings on a Pebble device, though, this is possibly the source of the original confusion.
